Creamy Shrimp Pasta
  • Ingredients:
    Shrimp - Butterfly Pasta - Boiled veggies - seasoning
Dice and boil potatoes, carrots, peas and green beans until 80% cooked. In a pan, put some olive oil, add the shrimp, season with black pepper salt and coriander then stir until shrimp color turns pink. Take shrimp out then add one diced onion, garlic and parsely and stir for 5 minutes. add the vagatables, shrimp and heavy cream. stir then add the pasta.
